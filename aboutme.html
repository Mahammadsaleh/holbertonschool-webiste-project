About Me

Greetings! I am Mahammadsaleh Abbas, a dedicated individual with a strong background in Computer Information Systems and practical experience as a Junior Full-Stack Developer. I am excited to begin my journey in the field of technology and web development.

I have recently obtained a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems from Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University. Furthermore, I completed a Full-Stack development programme at the Israel Azerbaijan Training Centre, where I honed my skills in various technologies.

As a developer, I have showcased my proficiency in JavaScript, React.js, Next.js, and skillfully utilized Firebase for data management. Additionally, I possess expertise in desktop application development using C# and Windows Forms. My proficiency in frontend development encompasses HTML5, CSS (including Tailwind and Bootstrap5), along with design tools like Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma.

Apart from these technical skills,I'm familiar with Node.js, Python, SQL, Git, REST API, and Redux. Additionally, I hold significant knowledge of the Windows operating system and troubleshooting, ensuring a seamless IT operation.

As a dedicated front-end developer, my primary objective is to craft user-friendly and visually appealing web experiences that captivate and engage users. However, my passion for technology goes beyond the frontend. I'm deeply fascinated by the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI). I firmly believe that AI will shape the future of technology and revolutionize industries. I am committed to being a part of this exciting journey. As a programmer, I aim to seamlessly merge my frontend development skills with AI, contributing to innovative solutions that not only enhance user experiences but also drive meaningful change in the tech world. I'm excited to explore this dual path, leveraging my creativity in frontend design and my fascination with AI to create solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible.
